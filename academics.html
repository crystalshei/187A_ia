University of Louisiana Lafayette offers a range of engaging majors for undergrads in award-winning departments. In addition, the University's Honors Program offers serious and motivated undergraduated students extra access to enhanced resources and opportunities for their continued academic success.

Graduate:

Graduate students are a vital part of the community of scholars at UL Lafayette, and we strive to create an environment that promotes scientific inquiry and stimulates the creative process. In collaboration with the academic deans, program coordinators, and the graduate council, we provide leadership and vision for graduate education. The university's academic colleges offer a substantial variety of both master's and doctoral level degree programs aimed at preparing the next generation of scholars, researchers, practitioners, and leaders in both the private and public sector.
