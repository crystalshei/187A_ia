Bryan James: Prospective Student

Bryan is a high school senior, and he's currently looking for colleges that he is interested in applying to. His parents were both born and raised in Louisiana, and after visiting the state a number of times, Bryan has decided he would like to attend college near his extended family in Louisiana. However, since he is not a Louisiana resident, he knows he must pay out-of-state fees. Bryan is currently the president of his high school's robotics team, and has developed a passion for creating and building various types of machines. Eventually, he wants to earn a PhD in Mechanical Engineering.

SCENARIOs

Information: Scenario #1
Context Bryan wants to know what major he could consider applying for.
Question What Engineering majors are there?
User Needs Bryan needs to look through the list of majors available at the university.
User Steps 1. Enter URL "http://www.louisiana.edu/" 2. Click on "Prospective Students" tab in the vertical menu near the middle of the page 3. Click on "Freshman Students" 4. Click on "Academic Colleges/Majors" under the "More Information" section 5. Click on "Engineering" link under "Degree Programs by College" section
Information: Scenario #2
Context Bryan wants to know how much he will have to pay per year, as an out-of-state student.
Question How much would I have to pay per year?
User Needs Bryan needs to find information about fees and costs.
User Steps 1. Enter URL "http://www.louisiana.edu/" 2. Click on "Prospective Students" tab in the vertical menu near the middle of the page 3. Click on "Freshman Students" 4. Click on "Estimated Costs" link under the "Money Matters" section
Information: Scenario #3
Context Bryan wants to know whether he meets all the requirements for admission into the university.
Question Do I qualify to be admitted into this university?
User Needs Bryan needs to look at the admission requirements.
User Steps 1. Enter URL "http://www.louisiana.edu" 2. Click on "Prospective Students" tab in the vertical menu near the middle of the page 3. Click on "Freshman Students" 4. Click on "Admission Requirements" link under "Admissions Information" section
