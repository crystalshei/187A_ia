User Persona 1

Graham Winchester: Prospective Parent

Graham's daughter Jill is a high school senior who is considering applying for the University of Louisiana at Lafayette. He hasn't heard much at all about the campus, so he has decided that he would like to take his daughter to visit the campus before she applies. In addition, Graham would like an overall price estimate about what his daughter's fees would add up to.

SCENARIOS

Information: Scenario #1
Context Graham wants to read up on some quick facts about the university.
Question Overall, what's the university like?
User Needs Graham needs to find general information about the university.t
User Steps 1.Enter URL "http://www.louisiana.edu/" 2. Click on "about us" tab in the top navigation bar 3. Click on "Fast Facts about UL Lafayette""
Information: Scenario #2
Context Graham wants to look at and print out the campus map for his and his daughter's campus visit.
Question What's the campus layout?
User Needs Graham needs to find the campus map.
User Steps "1. Enter URL "http://www.louisiana.edu/" 2. Click on "about us" tab in the top navigation bar 3. Click on "Campus Map"
Information: Scenario #3
Context Graham wants to know around how much he'll be paying if his daughter ends up going to UL Lafayette
Question How much will I have to pay each year if my daughter attends this university?
User Needs Graham needs to find out an estimated cost of how much all university expenses for his daughter's college experience will cost.
User Steps 1. Enter URL "http://www.louisiana.edu/ 2. Click on "about us" tab in the top navigation bar 3. Click on "Net Price Calculator" and fill out the form provide
