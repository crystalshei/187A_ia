User Persona 5

Michelle Hanks: Faculty Member

Michelle is a first-year Professor in Biology at UL Lafayette, and moved into the area with her husband and toddler just before the new school year started. In addition to wanting to gain familiarity with the campus, Michelle wants to take her freshman seminar class on a field trip to the zoo.

SCENARIOs

Information: Scenario #1
Context Michelle wants to know where she can bring her toddler to daycare while she and her husband are at work.
Question Where can I drop my child off in the mornings?
User Needs Michelle needs to find out if there's some sort of childcare for faculty members.
User Steps 1. Enter URL "http://www.louisiana.edu/" 2. Click on "Faculty & Staff" tab in the vertical menu near the middle of the page 3. Click on the "Childcare" link under the "Services & Facilities" section
Information: Scenario #2
Context Michelle has heard about university's art museum, and wants to know more general information about it, including the cost.
Question What sorts of exhibits are there at the art museum, and how much do I need to pay to get in?
User Needs Michelle needs to find general information about the art museum.
User Steps 1. Enter URL "http://www.louisiana.edu/" 2. Click on "Faculty & Staff" tab in the vertical menu near the middle of the page 3. Click on the "University Art Musemu: Faculty/Staff Enter FREE" link
Information: Scenario #3
Context Michelle wants to take her Biology class to the zoo, and needs to know what the university's field trip policy is.
Question What do I need to do if I want to take my students on a field trip to the zoo?
User Needs Michelle needs to find information on what the university requires of faculty when it comes to taking classes on field trips.
User Steps 1. Enter URL "http://www.louisiana.edu/" 2. Click on "Faculty & Staff" tab in the vertical menu near the middle of the page 3. Click on the "Faculty" link in the "Handbooks (Faculty & Staff)" item under the "Administration & Policies" section 4. Click on "Instructional and Research Policies" 5. Scroll down to "Class Field Trips" section on second page
