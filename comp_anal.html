Final Nav Section Selections

After some careful consideration, we decided on the dividing the site into the following main navigation sections and subsequent subsecitons. For the "information for" pages such as prospective students. current students, parents, etc. we decided to have a seperate but (slightly) less visible navigation bar on the top right of the page. We did this because we thought that the "information for" pages were simply too important to shove into a dropdown menu and wanted to remain always visible no matter where you went. As for the main sections, we chose those six (although it really is five) because we felt that those sections adequately cover all subsections we'll want to cover. We feel this is a huge improvement over the original Louisiana website because it is much more stream lined and efficient.
