About our program:

UL Lafayette boasts creative faculty and effective research centers focused on specific challenges to society. The research does this through the following strategies: engaging in interdisciplinary research that includes students; developing solutions to real-world problems; and nurturing, preserving and expressing the region's Cajun and Creole cultures. We welcome you to explore the pages and linkages of our Research Office web site to appreciate the unique research climate and regional culture that explains our excitement in developing the new research university (adapted from Vice President of Research Robert R. Twilley's statement)
