Rachel Lee: Alumnus

Rachel graduated from UL Lafayette 9 years ago, and participated in many student organizations throughout her time there. (This why she likes to check up on how the university is doing from time to time.) As a former member of the university's Women's Basketball Team, Rachel likes to attend the Women's Basketball games to cheer them on. In the past few years, she's finally worked her way up the corporate ladder, she wants to give something back to her old university.

SCENARIOs

Information: Scenario #1
Context Rachel wants to catch up on recent and current happenings at UL Lafayette.
Question What's new at my old university?
User Needs Rachel needs to find news or updates about the university.
User Steps 1. Enter URL "http://www.louisiana.edu/" 2. Click on "Alumni, Donors & Friends" tab in the vertical menu near the middle of the page 3. Click on "Public Relations & News Services" section 4. Click on "News & Events" 5. Read through & click on news articles about the university
Information: Scenario #2
Context Rachel wants to go to a UL Lafayette Women's Basketball game.
Question When are the upcoming home games?
User Needs Rachel needs to find the Women's Basketball Team schedule.
User Steps 1. Enter URL "http://www.louisian.edu/" 2. Click on "athletics" tab in the top navigation bar 3. Click on the "Ragincajuns.com" link 4. Mouse over "SCHEDULES" in the top navigation bar 5. Go down the dropdown menu and click "Basketball (W)"
Information: Scenario #3
Context Rachel wants to give a gift to the university since she can finally comfortably afford to.
Question What sorts of gifts can I give to UL Lafayette?
User Needs Rachel needs to find information on giving gifts to the university.
User Steps 1. Enter URL "http://www.louisiana.edu/" 2. Click on "Alumni, Donors & Friends" tab in the vertical menu near the middle of the page 3. Click on "Office of Development" 4. Click on the links under the "Where Your Gift is Needed" section to read about specific types of gifts
