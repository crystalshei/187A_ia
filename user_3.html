Jane Puentes: Current Student

Jane is a current student at the University of Louisiana at Lafayette. She is a third-year Psychology student who get average grades. While she is not directly involved with any campus organizations, Jane likes to attend various university events with her roommates. To escape the stress that comes with being an academic student, she enjoys playing sports with her friends in her free time.

SCENARIOs

Information: Scenario #1
Context Jane wants to sign up for Intramurals with her roommates.
Question What intramurals are being offered?
User Needs Jane needs to look through the list intramurals.
User Steps 1. Enter URL "http://www.louisiana.edu/" 2. Click on "Current Students" tab in the vertical menu near the middle of the page 3. Scroll down to and click on "Recreational Sports" header/link 4. Click on "Intramurals" tab on vertical menu on the left side of the page
Information: Scenario #2
Context Jane has decided not to study for the rest of the day, and wants to know if there are any University Program Council events she can attend.
Question Does the University Program Council have anything going on tonight?
User Needs Jane needs to view the University Program Council's page
User Steps 1. Enter URL "http://www.louisiana.edu/" 2. Click on "Current Students" tab in the vertical menu near the middle of the page 3. Scroll down to "Campus Organizations" section and click on "University Program Council"
Information: Scenario #3
Context Jane wants to plan her class schedule for the upcoming semester.
Question What will my class schedule look like next semester?
User Needs Jane needs to find the class schedule for the upcoming semester, and also check what days and times the classes she plans on taking are being offered.
User Steps 1. Enter URL "http://www.louisiana.edu/" 2. Click on "Current Students" tab in the vertical menu near the middle of the page 3. Click on "Schedule of Classes" link (the first link in the list) 4. Scroll down to the year she is looking for 5. Click on the link within the bullet point labeled "The link to the List of Courses:" 6. Enter "User Name" and "Password." 7. Note: this step is a general assumption of what Jane needs to do, since we cannot access the class schedule (no User Name and Password). Search for her classes depending on what she knows she needs to take
